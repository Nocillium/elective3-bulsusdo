bulacan state university About sports development

Sports Development Office is one of the units under the umbrella of Student Affairs and Services which caters the student’s development services of Bulacan State University. This provides assistance in discovering ones potential in different sports discipline, assist in improving and training of athletes. Responsible in providing assistance thru careful selection of the coaching and training staff.

operational procedures

The varsity members of each respective team need to observe & follow guidelines and by laws of the following considerations:

•  Recruitment of athletes are thru the application of the students or thru the Recommendation of the coach.

•  Strict Compliance of the varsity members to Summer training & semestral training & Sports clinic.

•  Provides scholarship grants whether full or half for deserving athletes as they are classified based on their participation to higher meets like SCUAA, UCLAA & other pocket competition on top of their regular year round training & thru the recommendation of the coaches.

•  Full Scholarship privileged are for those athletes who maintain no failing more than 10% of their SCUAA enrolled units, participated in SCUAA sanctioned based on the gravity of offense mode.

•  Athletes may be allowed to stay in the athlete’s dorm after deliberation of the coaches, dorm home dean & sports director.

•  Invitation in different community initiated games will pass to the office of the sports head and the approval be based as they pass the standard requirements like permits medical permit & parental consent.

This office provides opportunities for the students who have exceptional talents in different sports disciplines. This office function:

1. Recruiting agent for student deserving to be varsity members.

2. Implementor of rules and regulations needed for training of athletes.

3. Endorsing unit for athletic scholarship grants classified as full scholarship and half scholarship of athletes.

4. Organizer of game competition.

5. Coordinator for pocket competition, regional competition, national competition and even to an international competition.

6. Supervisor for the conduct, discipline and total behavior of athletes.

Health Care

The designee (Coaches and Trainer) shall be responsible for the administering the following policies and procedures pertaining to health care. (Annually every first semester of the year) 

•  Individualized Student-Athlete Care (New/Old Athletes). Athlete Training Program shall provide individualized student-athlete care that includes:

(a) performing assessments of health status, 

(b) making physician referrals for diagnosis and health care strategy instructions, (c) developing health care plans based on physician diagnosis and instructions, (d) implementing health care treatment and 

(e) evaluating the success of health care interventions.

Health Care Intervention. Athlete Training Program shall implement health care interventions targeted at returning student-athletes to pre-injury performance levels as quickly and as safely as possible with maximum attention to prevention of reoccurrence resulting from incomplete rehabilitation.

Duties and Responsibilities

Head

1. Plan, implement, monitor and coordinate all recreational and sports program of the University.
2. Recommend recruited athletes for admission to the University.
3. Check training programs prepared by coaches, asst. coaches.
4. Monitor athlete’s over-all performance in training, competition and studies.
5. Ensure the safety of the athletes during trainings and competition. 
6. Coordinate with the proper authorities’ activities and competition, the team plans to join in.
7. Identify materials to be purchased for the team thru cooperation of the coaches, asst. coaches, trainer and even team captain.
8. Check the different University privileges for varsity player, if properly delivered.
9. Acts as the chair of discipline committee for the varsity players.
10. Tap government or non-government agencies to support sports projects initiated by the University.
11. Recommend request to participate in different recreation and sports activities.
12. Approved dormitory privileges thru the recommendation of the coach.
13. Select athletes for its participation to higher meet.

Coach

1. Recruit athletes.
2. Gives instruction in playing strategies and techniques.
3. Impose discipline to improve the quality of the team member.
4. Scout competition for proper exposure of the team.
5. Recommend athletes for scholarship privileges.
6. Submit training attendance to the head of sports development unit for stipend
7. Endorse qualified athletes for dormitory privileges.
8. Turn over request of the trainer for material such as playing equipment, supplies including athlete’s apparel.
9. Acts as parent/guardian to the athletes during training and game proper.
10. Recommends sanction of erring athletes to the board of discipline will be headed by the sports head, asst. coach, trainer and two other coaches of other team.
11. Show dignity and fairness in his/her action which will affect the whole team 
12. Set example commendable of a coach.
13. Help athletes discover his strength in the physical, maturity in his social mental, emotional and spiritual well-being.
14. Assist the sports head in all undertakings concerning training of athletes and sports program development.
15. Receives instruction from the sports head re: planning, supervision and coordination operation and evaluations of the sports training program.
16. Prepares training program that develops the competitiveness of the athletes.
17. Work closely with the trainer in implementing the designed training program.
18 Ensure safety of the athletes both in training and competition.
19 Coordinates with the proper authorities all competitions the team plans to join in.

Assistant Coach
 
1. To support or suggest all activities which will improve the athlete’s coordination may be initiated by the coach.
2. Assist the coach and support the trainer on their assign task.
3. In absence of the coach asst. coach assumes all the responsibilities entrusted to the coach.

Trainers 
 
1. Prepares training program that develop the competitiveness of the athletes.
2. Execution of the training program activity.
3. Ready athletes for mental toughness experience.
4. Programming game plans and tactics prior to a game
5. Endorse qualified for team composition.
6. Counter check attendance and participation of both varsity members and training poll.
7. Ensure all equipment materials are properly for competition.

Chaperon

1. To provide company to the athletes during competition.
2. To report untoward behavior of athletes to the right authority.
3. To guide athletes in the conduct and behavior during off game.

Secretary /Clerk 

1. Payroll of sports development personnel.
2. Keeping time record
3. Preparing of sports development communications.
4. Submission of request, travel order and estimates
5. Serving waiver to the athletes and officials who are to travel.

Property Custodian

1. Check, monitor SDO supplies and materials.
2. Issues and receive materials to the team before and after training.
3. Regular inventory of sports equipment and materials 
4. Ensure supplies, sports material and equipment are in good condition.
5. Return all condemnable material and equipment.
6. Dormitory monitoring (occupancy and cleanliness). 
7. Monitoring of all training facilities (readiness and cleanliness).
